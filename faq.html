FAQ

Are The Drivers Trustworthy?

Trust in our drivers is paramount to us. We take immense pride in our rigorous selection process, ensuring that each driver is not only highly skilled but also embodies integrity and reliability. Our drivers undergo extensive background checks and comprehensive training to guarantee they uphold the highest standards of professionalism and safety. With us, you can rest assured that your trust is well-placed, as our drivers are committed to providing a secure and dependable service every time.

Why Do The Drivers Record Interactions?

Recording interactions is a safety measure we've implemented to protect both our drivers and passengers. These recordings serve as a means of ensuring accountability and providing an added layer of security in case of any disputes or unexpected situations during the ride. The recordings are strictly for internal use and are handled with the utmost confidentiality, in line with privacy regulations. This practice enables us to maintain a safe and reliable service, fostering an environment where everyone feels secure throughout their journey.

Does Price Vary On Distance?

Yes, our pricing structure is designed to account for distance traveled. The fare is calculated based on the distance of the journey, ensuring fairness and transparency in our pricing. This approach allows us to provide competitive rates while accurately reflecting the distance covered during the ride. Rest assured, our pricing model aims to offer a fair and reasonable cost that aligns with the distance traveled, delivering value and convenience to our passengers.
